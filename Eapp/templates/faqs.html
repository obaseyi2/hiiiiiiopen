Frequently Asked Questions

TRANSACTIONS

 What Methods Are Available for Transactions? Transactions are conducted in person between the buyer and seller. This marketplace facilitates contact between both parties, but the actual exchange happens offline, either through meetups or other agreed-upon means.
Are Payments Made Through the Website? No, there are no payment options on the website. Buyers and sellers must agree on how to complete transactions, but it's advised that no payments should be made until both parties have met and inspected the item.
Is It Safe to Buy Items Here? Yes, as long as you follow the recommended safety measures. Always meet in a public space or at a location on campus. Ensure you inspect the product before handing over any money. The marketplace does not facilitate or process payments, so be cautious.

SAFETY & LIABILITY

 Is This Marketplace Liable for Scams? The marketplace is not liable for any scams or disputes that may occur. It's essential to only meet sellers in person and avoid paying for any product you haven't seen and inspected. Always take precautions during transactions.
What Should I Do if I Suspect a Scam? If you suspect any suspicious activity, avoid the transaction and report the seller to the website administrators. It's advised to never send money to anyone before you’ve met them in person and verified the product.

LISTING & CONTACT

 How Do I List an Item for Sale? Listing an item is easy! Simply upload a picture of your item, provide a description, and include your contact information. Interested buyers will contact you directly to arrange the sale.
Do I Need an Account to Buy or Sell? Yes, both buyers and sellers need to create an account to use the marketplace. This allows for easier communication and management of listings.
How Do I Contact a Seller? Once you've found an item you're interested in, click on the listing and you'll see the seller's contact information. Reach out to them directly to arrange a meeting.
